# Haus of Balloons — SEO & Discoverability Pass

**Date:** 2026-06-04
**Goal:** Make the site easily found on Google (local search) *and* surfaced by
LLM/AI assistants. Owner's stated top priority.

---

## What was implemented

### `index.html` `<head>`
- Keyword + location **title**: "Balloon Garlands & Decor · Toronto & Vancouver | Haus of Balloons".
- **Meta description** (~155 chars) with services + service area + CTA.
- **Canonical** `https://hausofballoons.ca/`, explicit `robots: index, follow, max-image-preview:large`, `theme-color`.
- **Open Graph** + **Twitter** `summary_large_image` (title, description, url, 1200×630 image, alt, `en_CA`).
- **Favicons**: `/favicon.svg` (+ `favicon-32.png` fallback) and `apple-touch-icon.png`.
- **JSON-LD** (`@graph`): `WebSite` + `LocalBusiness` — name, description, url, logo, image, email, `priceRange`, `sameAs` (Instagram), `areaServed` (GTA, Toronto, Greater Vancouver, Vancouver), `knowsAbout`, and an `OfferCatalog` of the 4 packages with starting prices in CAD.
- `<html lang="en-CA">` and a keyword-rich screen-reader `<h1>`.

### New root files
- **`robots.txt`** — allows all crawlers and *explicitly welcomes* the major AI crawlers (GPTBot, OAI-SearchBot, ChatGPT-User, ClaudeBot, Claude-User, Claude-SearchBot, PerplexityBot, Perplexity-User, Google-Extended, Applebot, Amazonbot, CCBot) + `Sitemap:` line.
- **`sitemap.xml`** — homepage only (the `/thank-you` page is `noindex`).
- **`llms.txt`** — concise, structured business summary (emerging convention for AI assistants): description, packages + prices, how-it-works, contact/service area.

### Generated assets
- `assets/og-image.png` (1200×630) + `assets/og-image.svg` source — white card, red balloon accent, wordmark, tagline, "TORONTO · VANCOUVER". Built locally with `qlmanage` (SVG→PNG) + `sips` (center-crop).
- `favicon.svg`, `favicon-32.png`, `apple-touch-icon.png` — from the existing clean balloon SVG.

### `thank-you/index.html`
- Added favicons + `theme-color` (already `noindex`).

---

## Key decisions

- **Allow AI crawlers (don't block).** The goal is visibility, and there's no
  proprietary content to protect — so both training bots and search/citation bots
  are welcomed in `robots.txt` and via `llms.txt`.
- **One `LocalBusiness` entity with `areaServed` for both regions** (not separate
  per-city branches). Matches the owner's "one site for both locations, don't
  overthink it" guidance. No street address is published (none exists publicly);
  `addressCountry: CA` + `areaServed` carry the geo signal.
- **Generated a placeholder OG/share image.** Clean and on-brand, but a real photo
  of finished work will convert better in social previews — swap when available.
- **Prices** modeled as `minPrice` (they're "starting at") in the OfferCatalog.
